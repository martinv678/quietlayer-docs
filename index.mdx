QuietLayer is a verification and audit layer for UK compliance checks.
It automates verification workflows and produces inspection-ready audit packs that employers can rely on.

QuietLayer exists to remove ambiguity from compliance. Instead of manual screenshots, scattered PDFs, and ad-hoc record keeping. It provides a consistent, verifiable record of what check was performed, when it was performed, and what evidence was captured at the time.


### The problem QuietLayer solves

UK employers are legally required to carry out compliance checks.

In practice, these checks are often:

- Performed manually

- Documented inconsistently

- Stored across multiple systems

- Difficult to audit after the fact

- Easy to get wrong under time pressure

The result is operational risk: missed expiry dates, incomplete evidence, and reliance on human memory during audits.

QuietLayer addresses this by treating verification evidence as a first-class output, not an afterthought.

### What QuietLayer does

QuietLayer provides a structured way to run compliance checks and capture evidence at the moment the check is completed.

Today, QuietLayer:

- Runs a range of UK verifications

- Captures the result and supporting evidence at the time of the check

- Generates a structured audit pack for each verification

- Records timestamps and integrity hashes to protect against tampering

- Centralises verification history for employers and teams

The focus is not automation for its own sake, but durable, reviewable evidence.

### What QuietLayer is not

QuietLayer is intentionally narrow in scope.

It is **not**:

- A recruitment platform

- A background-check marketplace

- A document storage system

- A legal decision-maker

QuietLayer does not replace employer responsibility.
It provides the tooling and evidence needed to meet that responsibility with confidence.


### Who QuietLayer is for

QuietLayer is designed for organisations that run compliance checks as part of their operations, including:

- Employers conducting checks on candidates

- Recruitment agencies handling multiple candidates

- Operations and compliance teams exposed to audits

It is not intended for individuals running one-off checks or for jurisdictions outside the UK.


### How QuietLayer is delivered

QuietLayer is API-first.

This allows organisations to integrate verification directly into their existing workflows while maintaining a consistent evidence and audit model. A hosted interface is available where appropriate, but the underlying system is designed to be embedded, automated, and auditable.
